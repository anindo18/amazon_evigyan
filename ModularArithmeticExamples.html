I. Check whether the following relations are true or false

1) 17 ≡ 5 (mod 3)

Solution : 17 – 5 = 12

12 is a multiple of 3

∴ Congruence is true

2) 75 ≡ 10 (mod 15)

Solution : 75 – 10 = 65

3) 121 ≡ (-18) mod 17

Solution : 121 – (–18) = 139

139 is not a multiple of 17

∴ Congruence is false
