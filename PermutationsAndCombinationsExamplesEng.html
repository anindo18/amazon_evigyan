PERMUTATIONS

1) Write the meaning of 6P2

Solution : The number of arrangements of 6 different things taken 2 at a time is 6P2

2) Find the value of 5P3

Solution : nPr = n(n – 1) (n – 2) (n – 3) ..... (n – r + 1)

when n = 5, r = 3

5P3 = 5 (5 – 1) (5 – 2)

= 5 x 4 x 3

= 60
