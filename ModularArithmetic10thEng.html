Modular Arithmetic


1 Congruence of Numbers

We are familiar with the twelve hours clock. Although there are 24 hours in a day,the face of the clock shows only 12 hours.

We consider,

13th hour of the day is equivalent to 1st hour.

17th hour of the day is equivalent to 5th hour/etc.

This is true because repetition occurs after 12 single steps

This system is ‘modulo 12’

The relation is expressed as

13 Congruent to 1 (mod 12)

17 Congruent to 5 (mod 12)

In modular arithmetic, these relations are symbolically expressed as,

13 ≡ 1 (mod 12)

17 ≡ 5 (mod 12)

24 ≡ 0 (mod 12) etc.,
